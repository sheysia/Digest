Who We Are & What We Do

AI Digest by DMVoice

面向在 Washington D.C. 都会区（DMV）工作和生活的女性——有技术背景的工程师和开发者，也有非技术背景的政界、律师、医疗、金融分析等各领域专业人士。

共同点是：高等教育背景，每天使用着 AI，审慎然而积极面对。我们研究和探讨 AI 怎么真正帮到自己的工作和生活——在时代洪流中坚持涓涓清流，不贩卖焦虑，不堆砌术语。每周十分钟，留下真实的体验。

For professional women across the DMV — engineers, developers, attorneys, healthcare administrators, financial analysts, policy professionals, and beyond. United by advanced education, daily AI use, and a shared commitment to learning with intention, not anxiety. Ten minutes a week. Only what's real.

AI Digest

We start from life, not from technology. We don't ask "what model just dropped?" — we ask "Can this save me two hours?" "Can I use it without writing code?"

A clear signal in the noise — no hype, no jargon walls, no FOMO. Five minutes a week, only what's genuinely useful to you.

No one here will tell you "learn AI or get left behind." What you'll find is a circle of smart women, learning at their own pace, turning AI into a tool that works for them.

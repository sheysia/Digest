Day-of Timeline
CUSTOMIZE FOR YOUR EVENT
T-60 min
Arrive at venue. Unload decorations, set up tables, inflate balloons. Confirm food order / delivery time.
T-30 min
Layout table covers, plates, napkins, cups. Place goodie bags. Set up photo backdrop. Test music/speaker.
Start Time
Doors open! Greet guests, collect waivers (if venue requires). Guide to play area. Take group photos early while everyone's fresh.
+45 min
Transition activity — dance party, organized game, or venue-led activity. Good energy reset before food.
+60 min
Party room time! Sing Happy Birthday 🎶 → blow candles → cut cake → serve pizza & food. Take photos of cake moment!
+80 min
Open presents (optional — many families prefer opening at home). Distribute goodie bags to departing guests.
+100 min
Start cleanup. Pack leftover food. Collect decorations. Tip venue host. Final head count of presents/belongings.
+120 min
Venue checkout. Load car. Done! 🎉 Change kids into comfy clothes for the ride home.

Pro Tips
FROM PARENTS WHO'VE BEEN THERE
📸 Photo Strategy
Take group photos in the first 15 minutes — kids are cleanest and most cooperative. Assign one adult to be the "photographer" so you can focus on hosting. Snap decor before guests arrive.
🎁 Presents Protocol
Consider opening presents at home — it saves 15 min, avoids jealousy drama, and lets you properly note who gave what for thank-you cards. Bring a large bag/bin to collect gifts.
⚠️ Allergy Awareness
Ask about allergies on your RSVP. Flag allergy families visually on your check-in list. Notify venue staff. Keep allergy-safe snacks separate and clearly labeled. Many trampoline/bounce venues are nut-free — confirm beforehand.
💵 Host Tip
For venue parties with a dedicated host, $30–50 cash tip is standard for parties of 20+ kids. Hand it directly at checkout with a thank-you.
🧦 The Sock Reminder
If your venue requires socks (trampoline parks, bounce houses), mention it on the invitation AND send a reminder the morning of. Parents always forget. Keep 5 spare pairs in your bag.
🎈 Balloon Math
Helium balloons: 1 per kid + 10 for decor. Regular balloons for arches/clusters: buy 2x what you think. They pop. A hand pump is $5 and saves your lungs.
